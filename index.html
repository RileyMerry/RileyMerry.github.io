My Engineering Vision

I want my work to sit at the intersection of electrical engineering, software, communication systems, and human-centered design. I am not only interested in making systems work — I want to understand why they work, how they fail, and how they can be improved for the people using them.

My long-term goal is to contribute to advanced defense, robotics, communication, and embedded systems that help solve real operational problems. I believe engineering should be practical, creative, and explainable.
